**JurixAI** is an AI Legal Assistant designed to help lawyers, SMEs, and legal teams draft, review, summarize, and understand legal documents faster using AI-powered workflows, risk flags, and trusted source-based outputs.

> This repository is a public product blueprint. It does not contain confidential source code, private prompts, database design, client data, or investor-sensitive information.

---

## Problem

Legal work is often slow, expensive, and difficult to understand for many businesses and individuals.

Common problems include:

- Contracts are hard to read and interpret
- SMEs sign agreements without fully understanding risks
- Lawyers spend time on repetitive document review and drafting
- Legal teams need faster document summarization
- Users need plain-language explanations of legal documents
- Legal workflows are often fragmented across files, emails, and manual notes

---

## Target Users

JurixAI is being designed for:

- Lawyers and advocates
- Small and mid-size law firms
- SMEs and business owners
- Startup founders
- Legal consultants
- Compliance and operations teams
- Company secretaries and business advisors

---

## Core Use Cases

- Contract review
- Legal document summarization
- Risk flagging
- Legal notice explanation
- Drafting first versions of legal documents
- Clause extraction
- Case and matter workflow support
- Plain-English legal explanation
- Trusted citation-based legal research support

---

## MVP Scope

The first version of JurixAI should focus on a small number of high-value workflows.

### Must-Have Features

- Upload legal document
- Summarize document in simple language
- Extract key clauses
- Identify risk areas
- Highlight missing or unclear clauses
- Generate user-friendly explanations
- Provide structured legal review output
- Allow human legal review before final use

### Later Features

- Case management
- Client intake workflow
- Legal timeline builder
- Multi-document comparison
- Legal drafting assistant
- Jurisdiction-specific workflows
- Team collaboration
- Client dashboard
- Integration with legal databases

---

## AI Governance Approach

JurixAI should be built with responsible AI principles:

- Human-in-the-loop review
- Clear disclaimer that outputs are not final legal advice
- Source-based responses wherever possible
- Risk classification
- Audit-friendly output structure
- Data privacy controls
- Secure document handling
- User permission and access control

---

## Product Positioning

JurixAI is not designed to replace lawyers.

It is designed to support lawyers, legal professionals, and businesses by reducing repetitive work, improving document understanding, and helping users identify key legal risks faster.
