# Risk and Governance

Legal AI products require strong governance because legal documents can affect business, money, rights, obligations, and disputes.

JurixAI should be designed with safety, transparency, privacy, and human review.

## Key Risks

### 1. Incorrect Legal Interpretation

AI may misunderstand a clause or provide an incomplete explanation.

### 2. Missing Context

Legal meaning can depend on jurisdiction, facts, document history, and related agreements.

### 3. Over-Reliance on AI

Users may treat AI output as final legal advice.

### 4. Data Privacy

Legal documents may contain sensitive personal, commercial, or confidential data.

### 5. Source Reliability

Legal outputs should be supported by trusted legal sources wherever possible.

## Governance Principles

- Human-in-the-loop review
- Clear legal disclaimer
- Source-based output where possible
- Risk classification
- Secure document handling
- No use of confidential data for public examples
- Audit-friendly output structure
- Role-based access control in future versions
- Clear separation between AI assistance and legal advice

## Recommended Disclaimer

JurixAI output is for informational and workflow assistance purposes only. It is not legal advice. A qualified legal professional should review all outputs before any decision, response, filing, or signing.
