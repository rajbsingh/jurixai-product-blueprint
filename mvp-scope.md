# MVP Scope

The first version of JurixAI should focus on a small number of high-value legal workflows.

The goal of the MVP is not to build a full legal platform immediately. The goal is to validate whether users find value in AI-assisted legal document understanding, review, and drafting support.

## MVP Goal

Help users upload a legal document, understand it faster, identify risks, and receive a structured review summary.

## Must-Have MVP Features

- Upload a legal document
- Summarize the document in simple language
- Extract key clauses
- Identify obligations
- Highlight risky or unclear clauses
- Suggest missing clauses
- Generate a structured legal review output
- Add clear disclaimer that the output is not final legal advice
- Allow human legal review before use

## Nice-to-Have Later

- Case management
- Client intake workflow
- Multi-document comparison
- Legal drafting assistant
- Jurisdiction-specific legal workflows
- Team collaboration
- Client dashboard
- Legal database integration
- Workflow history
- Matter timeline tracking

## Avoid in MVP

- Full case management system
- Complex billing features
- Court filing automation
- Deep legal research engine
- Too many jurisdictions at once
- Fully autonomous legal advice
- Complex integrations before validation

## MVP Success Criteria

The MVP should prove that users are willing to use JurixAI for:

- Legal document summarization
- Contract review support
- Risk understanding
- Clause extraction
- Faster legal workflow preparation
