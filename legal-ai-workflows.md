# Legal AI Workflows

This file outlines high-level legal AI workflows that JurixAI may support.

## 1. Contract Review Workflow

User uploads a contract.

JurixAI analyzes the document and provides:

- Simple summary
- Key parties
- Important dates
- Payment terms
- Obligations
- Termination clauses
- Liability clauses
- Risk flags
- Missing or unclear clauses
- Questions for legal review

## 2. Legal Notice Explanation Workflow

User uploads or pastes a legal notice.

JurixAI provides:

- Plain-language explanation
- Key claims
- Required action
- Important deadlines
- Potential risks
- Suggested questions for a lawyer

## 3. Clause Extraction Workflow

JurixAI extracts important clauses such as:

- Payment
- Termination
- Confidentiality
- Liability
- Indemnity
- Governing law
- Dispute resolution
- Renewal
- Penalty
- Ownership

## 4. Drafting Support Workflow

User provides basic inputs.

JurixAI prepares a first draft for human review.

Possible documents:

- NDA
- Service agreement
- Employment agreement
- Vendor agreement
- Legal notice response
- Simple business agreement

## 5. Matter Support Workflow

For future versions, JurixAI may support matter-level workflows:

- Client intake
- Case ID creation
- Document collection
- Timeline tracking
- Task reminders
- Stage-wise notes
- Final outcome summary

## Human Review Principle

JurixAI should support legal professionals and users, but legal decisions should remain under human professional review.
