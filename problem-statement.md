# Problem Statement

Legal work is important, but for many individuals, startups, and SMEs, it is slow, expensive, and difficult to understand.

Many users sign contracts, legal notices, agreements, and business documents without fully understanding the risks, obligations, and consequences.

JurixAI is designed to explore how AI can support legal professionals and businesses by making legal document understanding faster, clearer, and more structured.

## Key Problems

- Legal documents are hard to understand for non-lawyers
- SMEs often cannot afford repeated legal review
- Lawyers spend significant time on repetitive drafting and document review
- Legal teams need faster summarization and clause extraction
- Business owners often miss risky or unclear clauses
- Legal workflows are fragmented across emails, files, notes, and manual processes
- Users need plain-language explanations before making decisions

## Opportunity

AI can support legal workflows by helping users:

- Summarize legal documents
- Extract key clauses
- Identify potential risks
- Understand obligations
- Prepare first drafts
- Organize legal workflows
- Support human legal review

## Important Note

JurixAI is not intended to replace lawyers. It is designed as an AI Legal Assistant to support legal professionals, SMEs, and legal teams with faster document understanding and structured legal workflows.
