# Target Users

JurixAI is being designed for users who regularly deal with legal documents, contracts, notices, compliance documents, and legal workflows.

## Primary Users

### 1. Lawyers and Advocates

Lawyers can use JurixAI to reduce repetitive work such as document summarization, clause extraction, first-draft preparation, and risk flagging.

### 2. Small and Mid-Size Law Firms

Small firms often have limited resources. JurixAI can help them improve productivity, manage document-heavy work, and deliver faster client support.

### 3. SMEs and Business Owners

Business owners often sign contracts without fully understanding risks. JurixAI can help them understand important clauses, obligations, and red flags before seeking professional legal advice.

### 4. Startup Founders

Founders regularly deal with vendor agreements, employment contracts, NDAs, shareholder documents, and compliance-related paperwork.

### 5. Legal Consultants

Independent legal consultants can use AI-assisted workflows to review, summarize, and prepare documents more efficiently.

### 6. Compliance and Operations Teams

Teams handling internal policies, vendor contracts, and regulatory documents can use JurixAI-style workflows to organize and understand legal information faster.

## Secondary Users

- Company secretaries
- Accountants
- Business advisors
- Freelancers
- HR teams
- Procurement teams
- Contract managers
