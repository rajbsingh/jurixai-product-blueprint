# Product Roadmap

This roadmap outlines a high-level direction for JurixAI.

## Phase 1: Product Validation

Goal: Validate demand before full development.

Activities:

- Define target users
- Interview lawyers, SMEs, and legal professionals
- Validate key pain points
- Create landing page
- Collect early interest
- Test sample document review workflows

## Phase 2: MVP

Goal: Build the first usable version.

Core features:

- Document upload
- Legal document summary
- Clause extraction
- Risk flags
- Plain-language explanation
- Structured review output
- Human review disclaimer

## Phase 3: Workflow Expansion

Goal: Move beyond single-document review.

Possible features:

- Legal notice explanation
- Drafting support
- Multi-document comparison
- Matter notes
- Client intake
- Review history

## Phase 4: Legal Operations Layer

Goal: Support legal professionals and teams.

Possible features:

- Case ID creation
- Matter tracking
- Stage-wise workflow
- Client dashboard
- Team collaboration
- Document library

## Phase 5: Enterprise and Compliance Readiness

Goal: Make JurixAI suitable for larger teams and regulated use.

Possible features:

- Role-based access
- Audit logs
- Secure storage
- Data retention controls
- Legal source integration
- Governance dashboard
- Admin controls

## Long-Term Vision

JurixAI aims to become a trusted AI Legal Assistant that supports legal professionals and businesses with faster document understanding, better risk awareness, and structured legal workflows.
